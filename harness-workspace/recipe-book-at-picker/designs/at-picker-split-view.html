Sichuan Classic 25 Mins

Yuxiang Shredded Pork
鱼香肉丝

kitchen Ingredients

  • Pork loin 300g, shredded
  • Wood ear mushrooms 50g, sliced
  • Carrots 1 medium, julienned
  • Pickled chili 2 tbsp, minced
  • Ginger & Garlic 1 tbsp each

The Editorial Take

Despite its name, "Fish-Fragrant" pork contains no seafood. It's a masterclass in Sichuan balancing: sweet, sour, spicy, and savory elements converging in a glossy, sun-drenched glaze that coats every tender strip of pork.

restaurant_menu Preparation Steps

1

Marinate the Pork

Thinly shred the pork loin and marinate with soy sauce, cooking wine, and a touch of cornstarch for 15 minutes.

2

The Aroma Base

Stir-fry pickled chili, ginger, and garlic in high heat until the oil turns a vibrant red-orange.

3

Flash Sear

Add pork shreds, wood ear, and carrots. Toss rapidly until just cooked, then pour over the 'Yuxiang' sauce mixture.
