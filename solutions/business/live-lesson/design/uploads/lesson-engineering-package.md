## 二、本课落实的课程标准（高中英语）
### 1. 课程标准依据
- **信息处理**：能提取、整理、概括稍长语篇的关键信息、主要内容、思想和观点，判断各种信息的异同和关联。
- **批判思辨**：能针对语篇的内容或观点进行合理质疑；能依据不同信息进行独立思考，评价语篇的内容和作者的观点，说明理由。

### 2. 英语学科核心素养对应
本课主要对应以下英语学科核心素养维度：
- **语言能力**：理解并使用与“beauty / standard / culture / identity / media / evaluate”相关的主题语言；能借助支架进行英文表达。
- **文化意识**：理解不同时代、不同文化中的审美差异，避免用单一标准判断“美”。
- **思维品质**：在对比历史与文化案例的基础上形成解释、判断与评价。
- **学习能力**：迁移使用 predicting、skimming、scanning、evaluating 等阅读策略，形成可复用的阅读路径。

### 3. 本课落实点
本课聚焦落实以下几点：
1. 学生能够运用标题预测、略读、寻读等阅读策略，理解语篇结构；
2. 学生能够从长语篇中提取不同时空下关于审美观的信息，并进行结构化整理；
3. 学生能够基于文本事实提出观点，对“单一且肤浅的审美”进行批判性评价，并说明理由；
4. 学生能够在 AI 支架帮助下，把“读懂文本”转化为“用英语表达判断”。

## 三、教材与语篇内容定位
### 1. 语篇主题
本课围绕 **Ideal Beauty** 展开，通过古今中外不同文化中的审美标准，探讨“美”的观念是否具有唯一性，并引导学生思考现代媒体塑造的审美标准是否单一而肤浅。

### 2. 语篇价值
本语篇的教学价值不仅在于语言理解，还在于：
- 通过跨时空材料建立比较意识；
- 通过文化差异弱化单一审美焦虑；
- 通过文本证据支持个人观点；
- 通过策略训练提升学生处理长语篇的能力。

### 3. 语篇结构定位
原文整体结构为典型议论/说明型语篇：
- 从现实冲突现象导入；
- 再从历史角度展开；
- 再从文化角度展开；
- 最后得出总结与价值判断。

### 4. 本课保留的原学习任务
本次 AI 改写 **完整保留** 原教案中的五个学习任务：
1. 图式激活与阅读预期建立 (Schema Activation)
2. 略读与语篇结构解码 (Structural Decoding)
3. 信息提取与多维矩阵构建 (Scanning & Matrix Building)
4. 证据锚定与批判质疑 (Evidence-Anchored Critical Thinking)
5. 元认知复盘 (Metacognitive Wrap-up)

- 学生具备一定高中英语阅读基础，能够完成基础略读与寻读任务；
- 学生对“beauty”这一主题有生活经验，但未必具备从历史、文化角度看审美差异的意识；

  - 能理解，但不敢说；
  - 能说关键词，但说不成句；
  - 能表达态度，但证据不足；
  - 能给出证据，但缺少逻辑连接词。

## 五、本课AI教学目标
本节课结束时，学生能够：
1. 运用标题预测与略读策略，梳理文章从“现象导入”到“历史纵比”，再到“文化横比”，最后“得出结论”的语篇结构；

5. 在 AI 提示下使用基本的学术表达支架，如 **Based on the text... / One example is... / This shows that... / In my opinion...**。

## 八、学习任务1：图式激活与阅读预期建立 (Schema Activation)
### 对应原任务内容
- 围绕标题 **Ideal Beauty** 激活背景经验；
- 快速提取前两段核心冲突：
  - 尼日利亚女孩 Happiness Edem 为了美而增肥；
  - 现代媒体强调瘦、白、宽肩等审美标准；
- 锁定全文核心问题：
  - *Is one idea of physical beauty really more attractive than another?*

### 任务目标
- 激活学生已有主题经验；
- 建立阅读预期；
- 抓住语篇开头制造的核心冲突；
- 形成初步问题意识：beauty may not be fixed.

### 任务引入脚本（可直接转语音）
> Let’s start with the title: **Ideal Beauty**.  
> When you hear these two words, what comes to your mind? Maybe fashion, appearance, media, body image, or personal taste. That is a good beginning.  
> But now I want you to read the first two paragraphs very quickly. Do not stop at every new word. Your job is not to understand everything. Your job is to find the conflict.  
> Please pay attention to two things: first, who Happiness Edem is and what she wants; second, what kind of beauty the modern media is promoting.  
> After that, I want you to tell me what big question the writer is really asking.

### 学生端任务
1. 快速阅读前两段；
2. 回答：
   - Who is Happiness Edem?
   - What kind of beauty does modern media promote?
   - What is the writer’s main question?

### 英语输出支架
- Happiness Edem is ...
- In her culture, beauty means ...
- Modern media promotes ...
- The writer seems to ask whether ...

### 预设学生回答
- Happiness Edem is a Nigerian girl who wants to put on weight.
- The media promotes slim, fair women and broad-shouldered young men.
- The writer asks whether one idea of beauty is really more attractive than another.

### AI对话推进
- 若学生只说出表层信息，追问：
  - Why is this conflict interesting?
  - What makes the writer confused?
- 若学生无法概括问题，AI可提示：
  - Look at the last sentence of Paragraph 2.
- 若学生能准确说出冲突，AI进一步引导：
  - So this text is not only about beauty. It is about different ideas of beauty.

### 过程性评价观察点
- 是否能从标题快速激活主题背景；
- 是否能抓住前两段的核心冲突；
- 是否能用较完整英文句表达核心问题。

### 通过标准
- 能说出两种相反的审美标准；
- 能定位作者提出的核心问题；
- 能用 1—2 句英文概括前两段的冲突。

### 任务总结脚本（可直接转语音）
> Great. In this first task, you have already found the central conflict of the text. On one side, we see a culture where gaining weight is connected with beauty, wealth, and power. On the other side, we see modern media promoting a very different standard.  
> This conflict gives us the key question for the whole text: **Is one idea of beauty really better than another?**  
> Now that you have found the big question, we are ready to move to Task Two and look at how the writer organizes the whole text to answer it.

## 九、学习任务2：略读与语篇结构解码 (Structural Decoding)
### 对应原任务内容
- 阅读第3至第8段首句与关键连接词；
- 提炼语篇骨架：
  - Paras 1-2: Phenomenon
  - Paras 3-4: History
  - Paras 5-7: Culture
  - Para 8: Conclusion

### 任务目标
- 掌握略读策略；
- 识别语篇总体结构；
- 理解议论文/说明文“总—分—总”的展开方式；
- 为后续信息提取建立结构框架。

### 任务引入脚本（可直接转语音）
> In Task One, you found the big question of the text. But strong readers do not read a long text sentence by sentence first. They first look for the **skeleton** of the text.  
> So now, please read Paragraphs 3 to 8, but do not read every word carefully. Just focus on the first sentence of each paragraph and on linking words such as *However*, *For instance*, and *It appears that*.  
> Your goal is simple: find out how the writer builds the text. What comes after the opening conflict? Does the writer move through time, through place, or through examples?

### 学生端任务
1. 略读第3—8段首句；
2. 标出关键词；
3. 完成语篇结构判断：Phenomenon / History / Culture / Conclusion。

### 英语输出支架
- Paragraphs ... are mainly about ...
- The keyword is ...
- The writer moves from ... to ...
- This part functions as ...

### 预设学生回答
- Paragraphs 3-4 are about history.
- Paragraphs 5-7 are about different cultures.
- Paragraph 8 is the conclusion.

### AI对话推进
- 若学生能判断结构，AI追问：
  - Why does the writer move from history to culture?
- 若学生混淆段落，AI提示：
  - Look at the words “history”, “different cultures”, and “It appears that”.
- AI帮助学生口头整理结构：
  - Phenomenon → History → Culture → Conclusion.

### 过程性评价观察点
- 是否能用 skimming 抓住段落首句信息；
- 是否能区分内容细节与结构功能；
- 是否能用英语概括某部分的功能。

### 通过标准
- 能说出全文四段式逻辑；
- 能指出 history / culture / conclusion 等结构信号；
- 能用英文完成基本结构概括。

### 任务总结脚本（可直接转语音）
> Well done. You have just practiced a very important reading strategy: **skimming for structure**.  
> Instead of getting lost in details, you first found the writer’s roadmap. The text starts with a conflict, then moves across history, then across cultures, and finally reaches a conclusion.  
> This means the writer is not giving only one opinion. The writer is building an argument step by step.  
> Next, in Task Three, we will go back into the details and collect the evidence that supports this structure.

## 十、学习任务3：信息提取与多维矩阵构建 (Scanning & Matrix Building)
### 对应原任务内容
- 用寻读策略定位不同时间/地点下的审美实践；
- 提取并整理历史证据与文化证据；
- 完成信息提取矩阵表：
  - Time/Place
  - Practice / What they do
  - Hidden Reason / Why

### 任务目标
- 练习寻读；
- 从长段文本中抓取关键信息；
- 用矩阵方式进行结构化整理；
- 为后续观点表达准备“证据库”。

### 任务引入脚本（可直接转语音）
> Now we move from the skeleton to the details. In these paragraphs, there are many places, times, customs, and reasons. If you try to translate every sentence, you may get lost. So we need a better tool.  
> Our tool in this task is the **Information Matrix**.  
> As you read, do not collect everything. Only collect three things: **where**, **what people did**, and **why they did it**.  
> This will help you turn a long and difficult reading passage into clear and usable information.

### 学生端任务
请完成信息矩阵：
- Time/Place
- Practice / What they do
- Hidden Reason / Why

建议提取内容包括：
- early 1600s Europe
- Elizabethan England
- Borneo
- Maori in New Zealand
- Myanmar
- Indonesia
- 其他文中出现的关键历史/文化案例

### 英语输出支架
- In [Place/Time], people ...
- They did this by ...
- This meant ...
- The hidden reason was ...

### 预设学生回答
- In Elizabethan England, people wanted pale skin because make-up was expensive and pale skin showed wealth.
- In Borneo, people had tattoos because tattoos recorded important life events.
- In Myanmar, women wore metal rings because it was considered elegant.

### AI对话推进
- 若学生提取信息不完整，AI追问：
  - What is the place?
  - What exactly did people do?
  - What did it mean in that culture?
- 若学生只说 practice，不说 reason，AI提示：
  - The matrix is not complete without the hidden reason.
- 若学生信息混淆，AI引导其回到对应段落重新寻读。

### 过程性评价观察点
- 是否能区分事实与解释；
- 是否能从文本中抓到关键信息而不过度依赖逐句翻译；
- 是否能把零散细节整理成结构化信息。

### 通过标准
- 能完成至少 5 条有效矩阵信息；
- 每条信息至少包含 where / what / why 三要素中的两项以上；
- 能口头说出 2—3 条完整信息句。

### 任务总结脚本（可直接转语音）
> Excellent. In this task, you did something very important: you turned long paragraphs into organized information.  
> You were not just reading words. You were extracting evidence.  
> Now you can clearly see that beauty practices are connected with history, culture, status, identity, and values.  
> This matrix will become the foundation for the next task, because in Task Four you will use these facts as evidence to question modern beauty ideals.

## 十一、学习任务4：证据锚定与批判质疑 (Evidence-Anchored Critical Thinking)
### 对应原任务内容
- 回看第2段中的 *shallow beauty ideals*；
- 利用矩阵中的历史文化事实作为论据；
- 批判现代媒体制造的单一审美焦虑；
- 回应文末问题：
  - *Does this mean that we are all beautiful in our own way?*

### 任务目标
- 将信息提取结果转化为观点表达；
- 训练批判性思维；
- 能够“有证据地表达立场”；
- 强化英语议论表达中的观点—证据—解释链条。

### 任务引入脚本（可直接转语音）
> Now comes the most important thinking task of today’s lesson. You already have your evidence in the matrix. The question is: **Can you use it?**  
> Go back to Paragraph 2. The writer describes the modern media’s beauty standard as **shallow beauty ideals**.  
> Do you agree with the writer? Why or why not?  
> This time, I do not want a short answer like “yes” or “no”. I want you to support your idea with facts from the text or from your real life. Your matrix is your evidence bank.

### 学生端任务
1. 回看 Paragraph 2 中的 *shallow beauty ideals*；
2. 结合矩阵中的证据表达观点；
3. 回答：
   - Do you agree that the media’s beauty standard is shallow?
   - Does the text suggest that we are all beautiful in our own way?

### 英语输出支架
- In my opinion, ...
- Based on the text, I think ...
- One example is ...
- This shows that ...
- Therefore, I agree / disagree that ...

### 预设学生回答
- I agree it is shallow because beauty changes across time and cultures.
- The media promotes only one standard, but the text shows many different ideas of beauty.
- The author believes beauty is not fixed and people can be beautiful in different ways.

### AI对话推进
- 若学生观点空泛，AI追问：
  - Which fact from your matrix supports that?
- 若学生只复述文本，AI追问：
  - What do **you** think? Do you see similar beauty pressure in real life?
- 若学生表达断裂，AI可提供支架句：
  - Based on the text, I think...
  - One example is...
  - This shows that...
- 若学生只有观点和例子，没有解释，AI追问：
  - So what does this example prove?

### 过程性评价观察点
- 是否能使用文本证据支持观点；
- 是否能形成“观点—证据—解释”的基本表达链条；
- 是否开始体现个人判断而不只重复作者观点。

### 通过标准
- 能表达明确立场；
- 能至少引用 1 条文本证据；
- 能用 3—4 句英文完成基本观点表达。

### 任务总结脚本（可直接转语音）
> Very good. In this task, you moved from reading to thinking. You did not just collect facts—you used facts to support a judgment.  
> That is a big step in academic reading.  
> You have seen that beauty is never as simple or universal as the media sometimes claims. History and culture show us many different standards, and this gives us the power to question modern beauty pressure.  
> Now we move to our final task, where you will review not only what you learned, but also how you learned it.

## 十二、学习任务5：元认知复盘 (Metacognitive Wrap-up)
### 对应原任务内容
- 回顾本课是如何一步步读懂长文章的；
- 复盘四个阅读策略步骤：
  1. Predicting
  2. Skimming
  3. Scanning
  4. Evaluating

### 任务目标
- 帮助学生意识到本课不仅学了文本内容，也学了阅读策略；
- 提升策略迁移意识；
- 建立“以后遇到类似长文也可以这样读”的方法感。

### 任务引入脚本（可直接转语音）
> Before we finish, I do not want you to think only about the topic of beauty. I also want you to think about **how** you read this text today.  
> Good readers do not depend on translation alone. They use strategies.  
> So now, let’s look back and name the steps we used. If you can remember the steps, you can use them again when you meet a new English article.

### 学生端任务
请回顾并复述本节课的四个策略步骤：
1. Predicting
2. Skimming
